Who: All Singles 31+

What: Game Night/ Family Home Evening

When: Generally the 1st Monday of each month; occasionally the 2nd Monday when conflicts occur. 7-9 pm

Where: The Relief Society room (the room on the southwest corner of the building) of the Lindon Stake Center: 1050 E 100 N, Lindon

Why: To have fun, meet new people, build stronger connections with others, and strengthen our testimonies.

How: Bring games and/or a snack to share, if you'd like. Also, invite others who might be interested!

We usually have a different theme each month to makes things even more interesting!

Agenda: The evening starts with a spiritual thought/message. Next up is a group game with everyone. Then we break off into smaller groups and play whatever game each table picks.

How this started: I was at an event for widows and widowers and commented how I'd love to have more events in the Lindon area. So someone asked me, "Well, why don't you start one?" Well, why not? We started out with widows and widowers but soon expanded this event to include all singles.
